PHID - MAKONGO

POTTERS HOUSE INTERNATIONAL DAR-ES-SALAAM (PHID) ni jumuiya ya Kikristo iliyojitolea kueneza upendo wa Kristo kupitia uinjilisti, kufundisha, na huduma za jamii. Iko Makongo, Dar es Salaam, tumejitolea kufikia kila roho kwa injili na kuwalea waamini kuwa wafuasi waaminifu wa Yesu Kristo. POTTERS HOUSE INTERNATIONAL DAR-ES-SALAAM (PHID) is a vibrant Christian community dedicated to spreading the love of Christ through evangelism, discipleship, and community service. Located in Makongo, Dar es Salaam, we are committed to reaching every soul with the gospel and nurturing believers to become faithful followers of Jesus Christ.

Kuhusu SisiAbout Us

POTTERS HOUSE INTERNATIONAL DAR-ES-SALAAM (PHID)

PHID - MAKONGO ni jumuiya ya Kikristo iliyojitolea kueneza upendo wa Kristo kwa njia ya uinjilisti, kufundisha, na huduma za jamii. Ikiongozwa na Mch. Timothy & Mary Mwitai, kanisa letu limejitolea kufikia kila roho kwa injili na kuwalea waamini kuwa wafuasi waaminifu wa Yesu Kristo. PHID - MAKONGO is a vibrant Christian community dedicated to spreading the love of Christ through evangelism, discipleship, and community service. Led by Rev. Timothy & Mary Mwitai, our church is committed to reaching every soul with the gospel and nurturing believers to become faithful followers of Jesus Christ.

MaonoVision

Kufikia kila roho kwa injili ya Yesu Kristo.To reach every soul with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

DhamiraMission

Kuinjilisti, kufundisha, na kuhudumia jamii kwa upendo na imani.To evangelize, disciple, and serve the community through love and faith.
