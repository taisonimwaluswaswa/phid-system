POTTERS HOUSE INTERNATIONAL DAR-ES-SALAAM (PHID) is a vibrant Christian community dedicated to spreading the love of Christ through evangelism, discipleship, and community service. Located in Makongo, Dar es Salaam, we are committed to reaching every soul with the gospel and nurturing believers to become faithful followers of Jesus Christ.
